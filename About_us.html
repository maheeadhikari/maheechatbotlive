About Us

Learn more about Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Institute Of Technology Tanakpur, our mission, vision, and values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality technical education that meets the highest standards of excellence, fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship in our students.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading institution in technical education, recognized globally for our academic rigor, cutting-edge research, and commitment to creating a sustainable future.

Our Values

We value integrity, excellence, and inclusiveness, striving to create an environment that nurtures personal growth, professional development, and social responsibility.
