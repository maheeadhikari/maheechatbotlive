Courses We Offer

We offer a variety of technical courses for students at Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Institute Of Technology

BCA

The BCA course is a full-time three-year (six semesters) Bachelor's Degree in Computer Application. The degree helps interested students in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Applications.

B.Tech CSE

The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering is a four-year program that focuses on computer systems, software engineering, and data processing, preparing students for a range of careers in the tech industry.

B.Tech AIML

The B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is designed to provide in-depth knowledge of AI and ML algorithms, preparing students for careers in these cutting-edge fields.

B.Tech Robotics

The B.Tech in Robotics is a multidisciplinary program that combines mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science to design and create intelligent robotic systems.

B.Tech Mechanical

The B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering covers the design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems, preparing students for a variety of engineering roles.

B.Tech Civil

The B.Tech in Civil Engineering focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects such as buildings, roads, and bridges, equipping students with the skills needed for the civil engineering industry.
